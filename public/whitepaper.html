01. What Is Trap Wars

Trap Wars is a peer-to-peer skill-based trading competition platform built on the Solana blockchain. Two traders enter. One wins. The better trader takes the pot.

This is not gambling. Trap Wars is a skill competition where outcome is determined entirely by trading performance — specifically, which player achieves the highest portfolio percentage gain over a fixed time window. Luck does not determine the winner. Your decisions do.

02. How It Works

  • Player 1 creates a battle and sets a wager amount in SOL and a time window (5 min, 15 min, 1 hour, or 4 hours).
  • A unique battle link is generated and shared with Player 2.
  • Both players connect their Solana wallets and deposit their wager into a Squads Protocol multisig escrow vault. Neither player can touch those funds until the battle ends.
  • At battle start, both portfolios are snapshotted — total USD value of all tokens and SOL in each wallet, priced via Jupiter Price API.
  • Both players trade freely during the time window using any Solana DEX or protocol they choose. Trap Wars does not control or restrict your trades.
  • When the timer expires, both portfolios are re-evaluated at final prices. The player with the higher percentage gain wins.
  • The winner receives the full pot minus the platform fee. Funds are released automatically from escrow.

03. Technology Stack

  • Solana Blockchain — All transactions, deposits, and settlements occur on Solana mainnet. Fast finality. Low fees.
  • Squads Protocol v4 — Industry-standard multisig infrastructure used by leading Solana protocols. Funds are held in a program-derived vault. No single party can unilaterally move escrow funds. Squads is independently audited.
  • Jupiter Price API — Real-time token pricing aggregated across all major Solana DEXes. Used for portfolio valuation at battle start and end. Best-in-class data on Solana.
  • Metaplex — On-chain NFT verification for Bando Kids and Trap Stars holder fee discounts.
  • Cloudflare Pages — Frontend deployed globally. No centralized server holding user data.

04. Scoring Methodology

The winner is determined by portfolio percentage gain from the moment both players deposit — not raw profit, not dollar value, not trade volume. Percentage gain is the only metric that matters. This creates a level playing field regardless of wallet size.

What Gets Tracked

  • At deposit, Trap Wars takes a full snapshot of each wallet: every SPL token balance and the total SOL balance.
  • All tokens and SOL are priced in USD via the Jupiter Price API, which aggregates real-time pricing across every major Solana DEX.
  • Total portfolio value at snapshot = (SOL balance × SOL/USD) + Σ(token amount × token/USD)

Score Formula

  • Scores are refreshed every 30 seconds during the battle.
  • Score = ((Current Portfolio USD − Starting Portfolio USD) / Starting Portfolio USD) × 100
  • Displayed as a percentage gain or loss from your starting position.
  • The entire wallet is scored — not just trades made during the battle. All existing holdings count.

Tie Breaker

  • In the event of an exact tie in percentage gain, Player 1 (the battle creator) wins.

Example

  • Player 1 starts with $500 portfolio. Ends at $560. Score: +12%
  • Player 2 starts with $2,000 portfolio. Ends at $2,200. Score: +10%
  • Player 1 wins — despite making less in absolute dollars, their percentage gain was higher.

05. Platform Fees

Trap Wars charges a 3% platform fee taken from the total pot at settlement. The winner receives the remaining 97%.

Bando Kids and Trap Stars NFT holders on Solana qualify for a reduced 1.5% fee. Holder status is verified on-chain via Metaplex at battle creation. No manual claims. No codes. Your wallet speaks for itself.

Example: Two players wager 1 SOL each. Total pot = 2 SOL. At 3% fee, winner receives 1.94 SOL. Platform receives 0.06 SOL.

Platform fees go to the Trap Wars treasury wallet. These fees fund ongoing development, infrastructure, and future rewards programs.
